# 👟 FLO: Omnichannel Growth Strategy through Predictive Analytics & ML

![Project Banner](01_FLO_Overview.png)

## 🎯 Business Problem: The Efficiency Gap
As Turkey’s largest footwear retailer, FLO faced a strategic hurdle: **Marketing spend was increasing, but ROI was stagnating.** The brand was using a "One-Size-Fits-All" approach for 20,000 customers, missing the nuances of channel behavior and long-term value.

**The Objective:** Transition from reactive discounting to a **Predictive Growth Engine** using RFM, CLTV (BG/NBD & Gamma-Gamma), and K-Means Clustering.

---

## 🛠️ The Analytical Ecosystem
1.  **Descriptive (RFM):** Rule-based segmentation to audit current customer health.
2.  **Predictive (BG/NBD & Gamma-Gamma):** Forecasting future purchase probability and monetary value.
3.  **Discovery (K-Means Clustering):** Unsupervised ML to uncover hidden behavioral archetypes.

---

## 🔍 Data Intelligence & Insights

### 1. The Revenue Backbone (Champions)
Our analysis identified a core group of "Champions" that acts as the company's financial spine.
* **Impact:** This segment accounts for **64% of total revenue**.
* **Insight:** With an average recency of only **40 days**, they are hyper-active but need "Value-Added" services rather than deep discounts to protect margins.

### 2. Machine Learning Discovery: "Lapsed Whales"
While RFM shows who *was* good, our **K-Means Model (Cluster 3)** identified "Lapsed Whales."
* **The Pattern:** Historically high-value customers who have gone cold in the last 6 months.
* **The Strategy:** High-priority "Win-Back" campaigns with exclusive "We Miss You" incentives.
